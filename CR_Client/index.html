Our Services

How it works!

The need for a viable certificate creation that is accessible from anywhere, difficulties in waiting in line to do get your certificates i.e.birth certificates, marriage certificates, appointments with a civil registrar,checking genuity of this certificates, taking out fast census, using the database of documents in case of lost,errors in documents, and graduation-list generation, difficulties in accessing civil registry information remotely and establishing a viable fast Platform experience for this process are some of the many issues Civil Registry APP handles in the civil registry millieu.

Civil Registry APP is FREE to the Institutions (Clients) using it. However some services will be charged to clients based on their request from the civil registrar. Some of these services certificate creation, Remote tracking, , Online Certificate verifications and more. Remember these paid services are OPTIONAL!

Birth Certificate Creation

This module provides a means for birth certificate creation for prospective clients in need either remote and local. This creation process is followed by an automatically generated birth certificate for clients who request by sending the necessary documents needed by the civil registrar. It also gives room for manual processing of this documents forms if need be. Moreover, once the documents are confirmed ,the civil registrar can automatically generate birth certificate with a unique generated signature that will be used to verify its genuity. You will be contacted by the civil registrar of which u will be able to do a verification before complete payment be made and document sent.



Marriage certificate Creation

This module has as main mission, to resolve all problems around producing marriage certificates. Since time was a main constraint when it come to marriages, we provide an opportunity for you to book a marriage appointment, one month before the day of the wedding. Once you click on the get started form and book an Appointment, we will inform you on file submission etc.This module also provides a possibility to set deadlines and reminders to both clients and registrars. It also provides an optional means to set penalties for late and uncomplied Payment requests. Most of our clients have found this penalty aspect very helpful in acheiving success as things are done on time.



Payments

Cash collection has always been a crucial and heptic procedure in most institutional milieus. This module allows for the certificates (Birth certificates, Consultation, Mariage Certicates) settings including Creations and Caution. The Civil Registry Payment Module comes with three (2) integrated highly secured payment channels: MTN Mobile Money And Orange Money for now. These payment channels make cash collection less daunting by facilitating remote payments. The module provides real time and detail statistics of financial transactions on the system

About Us

Why choose this platfom

Civil Registry is a 2 in 1 package developed by University of Buea , Computer Science to help eliminate most of the problems Civil Registries in Cameroon, in particular and Africa as a whole encounter. These problems range from producing Birth certificates, Marriage certificates thro and certificate tracking
A Web App: A platform to bring together clients and civil registrars remotely scattered around national and international territory, thus providing a means of contact to get a particular service done.
An Offline App: This package is a complement of the Web App allowing for the registry to manage their information locally. It also synchronizes changes with the Web counterpart making its access readily available for those involve (client and registrar).This also allow them work without connectivity
